## 🚀 Overview

ALERTIQ is a futuristic AI-powered industrial monitoring platform designed for next-generation Human Machine Interfaces (HMI).

Traditional industrial HMIs overwhelm operators with disconnected alarms and noisy telemetry. ALERTIQ transforms industrial sensor data into situation-aware, decision-ready intelligence using:

* 🧠 AI Incident Compression
* 🔮 Predictive Maintenance Intelligence
* 🏭 Digital Twin Visualization
* 📈 Real-Time Telemetry Monitoring
* 👷 Multi-Role Dashboards
* 🚨 Emergency Escalation Intelligence

The platform is designed as a hackathon-ready prototype for the ABB Accelerator challenge focused on next-generation industrial control systems.

---

# ✨ Key Features

## 🧠 AI Incident Compression

Traditional HMIs generate multiple disconnected alarms.

ALERTIQ intelligently correlates sensor anomalies and compresses them into a single actionable industrial incident.

### Example:

Instead of:

* Temperature High
* Pressure High
* Vibration High
* Energy Spike

ALERTIQ generates:

> Compressor Failure Risk Detected
> Recommended Action: Shutdown + Inspection

---

## 🔮 Predictive Maintenance Intelligence

The platform predicts machine risk before actual downtime occurs.

Features include:

* Failure probability estimation
* AI confidence scoring
* Estimated time before failure
* Preventive maintenance recommendations
